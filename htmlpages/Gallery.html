Most Popular

Glass Handicrafts

Glass handicrafts refer to a wide range of artistic and decorative items made primarily from glass. Artists craft different types of shapes by blowing hot air in them, cutting them and manipulating them to create unique and intricate pieces of handicrafts.

Wood Handicrafts

Wooden handicrafts are handmade objects crafted from wood, showcasing intricate designs and skillful craftsmanship. These items range from decorative pieces like sculptures and carvings to functional objects such as furniture, toys, and kitchenware.
